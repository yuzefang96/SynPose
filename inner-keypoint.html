In addition, we provide occlusion annotation for each joint, divided into self-occlusion (joint is occluded by other body parts) and occlusion (joint is occluded by other people or objects). The following is a demonstration video of the occlusion information:

Our generation pipeline is based on Script Hook V. Occlusion annotation is inspired by this mod.
